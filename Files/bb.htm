Basketball

  • Basketball is a team sport in which two teams, most commonly of five players each, opposing one another on a rectangular court, compete with the primary objective of shooting a basketball (approximately 9.4 inches (24 cm) in diameter) through the defender's hoop (a basket 18 inches (46 cm) in diameter mounted 10 feet.
  • Players in a basketball game have 5 assigned basketball positions: center, power forward, small forward, point guard, and shooting guard.
  • Robert Jaworski, a former basketball player for Ginebra, was a point guard player and was thought to be the best Filipino basketball player according to clutchpoints.com
